OUR HISTORY

2015

FIRST RESTAURANT

We opened our first restaurant on February 2. Our first restaurant was located in Split, Croatia. There was 4 employees at the moment, their names were Filip Bikić, Marin Tomić, Branko Radić and Karlo Rešetar.

2017

EXPANSION

Two years after the opening of first restaurant in Split, Croatia, we expanded our buisness to Zagreb. That was the year of the famous "Triple take" burger which pleased all of our customers with it special and unique taste. That was the year we became a brand.

2018

BECOMING INTERNATIONAL

June 7 we opened our first restaurant outside of Croatia. We decided to settle in Bulgaria, Sofia. That day the history was written and many bellies were full. Our brand became to expand across whole Balkan with or knew drive-in system and 24/7 delivery service.

2020

GOLDEN YEAR

Today we own more than 5 restaurants across 3 balkan countries and we are well known and worldly acceptable brand. We have vast variety of products and unique system of delivery and serving food for our customers.
